Easy Sausage Dip!

Description

This is a tasty sausage dip that is extremely easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sausage
  • 2 16 oz cream cheeze
  • 1 8 oz can rotel

Steps

  1. Cook sausage in a skillet and drain grease.
  2. Place sausage in a microwaveable dish.
  3. Add rotel and cream cheese.
  4. Microwave for 3 minutes, stiring occationally.
  5. Let cool, and enjoy!
