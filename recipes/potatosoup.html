Instapot Potato Soup

This potato soup is extremely easy, and delicious!


Ingredients

  • 32 oz bag of frozen Ore-Ida 'cubed' potatoes
  • 1 packet of onion soup mix packet
  • One 32 oz chicken stock
  • 1 can of cream of cheddar
  • 1 can of cream of chicken
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Add frozen potatoes, soup mix packet, and chicken stock to Instapot
  2. Press 'soup' button on Instapot and let steam out when time is up
  3. Stir in cream of cheddar and cream of chicken
  4. Add in heavy cream
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
  6. Let cool and enjoy!
  7. Tip: I usually add bacon, cheddar cheese, hot sauce and chives to mine
