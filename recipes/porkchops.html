Easy Baked Porkchops!

Description

These baked porkchops are super easy to make!


Ingredients

  • 4 one inch cut boneless porkchops
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons onion salt
  • 2 teaspoons pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Grease a baking sheet
  3. Place all ingredients in a ziplock bag, close, and shake
  4. Bake for 20 min or until temperature is >145 degrees
  5. Take out of oven, and let rest for 5 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
